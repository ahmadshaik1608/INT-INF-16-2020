Vishnu Manchu

CEO

As CEO of Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Institutions, it gives immense pleasure for me to bring in “RECALL“ – a portal in the interest of alumni folks. As alumni of Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Institutions, I am proud to have such an interactive arena specifically for alumni.

I genuinely believe our chairman's words – Alumni are knowledge assets of educational institutions and, Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Institutions is not an exception. We, the alumni are the brand ambassadors of our institutions, representing the ethics & values learned her in Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Institutions, in one way or another throughout our lives.

It is now on the alumni's shoulders, to take Sree Vidyanikethan 's reputation to the next level and let the victory of Sree Vidyanikethan be visible globally. It is also our responsibility to guide juniors by sharing our knowledge, achievements, experiences and happiness; which can enlighten them in various directions.

As an alumnus, I pledge that I am always there for Sree Vidyanikethan in every possible way so that it can be an eminent platform for knowledge delivery. While writing this message, I turned nostalgic, recalling my studenthood days. I take this as an opportunity to welcome all my student friends to give valuable suggestions and necessary inputs to make our “RECALL" lively.
